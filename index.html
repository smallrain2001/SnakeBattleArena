💡 Master Strategies

🎯 Coiling Technique

Circle smaller snakes to trap them, forcing them to crash into your body. This is the most effective hunting strategy for experienced players.

⚡ Speed Management

Use speed boost sparingly - only for escapes or critical attacks. Each boost costs you length, so timing is everything in the arena.

🍎 Food Priority

Target the glowing orbs from defeated snakes - they're worth much more than regular food and can rapidly increase your size.

🛡️ Defensive Play

Stay near the arena edges when you're small, and move to the center only when you're confident in your size and skills.

📋 Arena Rules

🎮 Objective: Grow your snake by eating food and defeating other players. Become the longest snake in the arena to claim victory and earn your place on the leaderboard.

⚡ Movement: Control your snake using mouse movement or arrow keys. Click or press spacebar for speed boost, but remember that boosting costs you length over time.

💀 Elimination: If your snake's head touches another snake's body, you're eliminated instantly. However, other snakes can crash into YOUR body safely - use this to your advantage!

🍎 Growth: Eat the colorful food scattered around the arena to grow. When other snakes are eliminated, they leave behind valuable glowing orbs worth much more than regular food.

🏆 Victory: There's no official "end" to the game - keep growing and climbing the leaderboard. Your best length is recorded and you can always try to beat your personal record!
